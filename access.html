This guide explains how to access the ENES Data Space service.

First of all, you need to register an account with Check-in to get access to EGI services. Once registered, you have to enroll into the ENES Virtual Organization used for management and security purposes.

If you are already registered, log in here, otherwise please follow the steps below to join the ENES Data Space.

Sign up for an EGI Account

If you don't have an EGI Account yet, you can register here using your institutional credentials or any other community identity providers from the EGI infrastructure. As part of this process you will be assigned a personal unique EGI ID which will be then used across all EGI tools and services.

For further details, please refer to the official EGI documentation.

Join the vo.enes.org VO

After your account is registered, click here to enroll to the ENES VO.

The "BEGIN" button will redirect you to a compilation form. Insert your motivation and agree to the Acceptable Use Policy before submitting your request.

Your petition will be handled by the VO manager as soon as possible and a notification will be sent by email once the request is approved.

Log in to the ENES Data Space service

You should be now able to log in to the service.

Authenticate to EGI Check-in to obtain an OAuth2 access token and enjoy the ENES Data Space service!
